Interested in joining our lab? We are continuously looking for talented and self-motivated individuals who share our passion for deep learning and computer vision. Positions are fully funded (100%, TVL-13) and are available to applicants from all around the globe. The candidates are expected to conduct independent research and at the same time contribute to the leadership of ongoing projects, guide master and bachelor students, and contribute to our teaching activities.

Prospective Ph.D. students (next deadline: November 15, 2023)
We are hiring Ph.D. students over the International Max Planck Research School for Intelligent Systems (IMPRS-IS). IMPRS-IS is a very active research community, with top researchers in various fields of machine learning, AI, and robotics. If you want to join us for your Ph.D., consider applying through the next call (the application is November 15, 2023). I recommend additionaly contacting me around the application deadline. In this case, [PHD-IntelliSensing] should be the first term of your email's subject.

Visiting Ph.D. students

Are you doing your Ph.D. in another lab but your research has a lot in common with ours? Then come visiting us! Contact me with your email subject starting with [Visiting-IntelliSensing]. We are looking forward to your stay!

Postdoctoral researchers

We are always looking for talented postdoctoral researchers. In this case, please contact me directly with your email subject starting with [Postdoc-IntelliSensing]. The ideal candidate should hold a PhD in Computer Vision / Deep Learning / Robot Perception or related field and have a track record of publications in top conferences (CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, AAAI, ICRA, IROS) as well as excellent problem solving skills and a strong motivation in conducting independent cutting-edge research. We look forward to your application!

Master and bachelor theses

If you are a student at the University of Stuttgart and are passionate about one of {machine learning, deep learning, computer vision} and want to apply what you have learned in the area of activity recognition / video comprehension in your thesis - please contact me with your subject starting with [Thesis-IntelliSensing], your a transcript of records and a few sentences about yourself.
